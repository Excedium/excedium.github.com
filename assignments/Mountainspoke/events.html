Cycle the Continental Divide

Welcome to everything you need to know about our yearly held Continental Divide get-away.

This year we are celebrating the 10 year anniversary and the experience will reflect that.

This 2 week trek will commence in Canada and traverse the United States, culminating in Mexico.

Due to the popularity we have amassed dozens of sponsors which will help keep the costs down.

Along with it being televised, this will be a journey you will never forget

The price if, you bring all your own equipment, is only $1,000

We have kits for those who want to use our equipment that will come with warranties and insurance.

We will be holding this event on the first week of September to keep those temperatures manageable.

And last but not least. As per usual, for those who place in the top 10 will receive a voucher for next year that will pay for it all!
